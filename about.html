How We Got Here

The 100CLAN story

From a Telegram group to a global creator platform — built by creators, for creators.

24
Early 2024
It Started in a Telegram Group

A small group of content creators in the Arab world started a private Telegram group to share collab opportunities. Within weeks, it had grown to over 300 members from across the region — creators who were hungry to work together but had no real infrastructure to do it.

24
Mid 2024
The Platform Takes Shape

The founders recognized that group chats couldn't scale the real problems: finding the right partners, structuring agreements, tracking results. Development began on a purpose-built platform with creator matching, a collaboration hub, and built-in analytics.

25
2025
Launch & Global Growth

100CLAN officially launched to the public. Creators from 45+ countries joined in the first six months. The platform facilitated thousands of successful collaborations, with creators reporting an average 34% increase in audience growth within 90 days of joining.

26
2026 — Today
12,000+ Creators Strong

Today, 100CLAN is the leading creator collaboration platform in the Arab world and growing globally. We've expanded to include deal management, brand partnership tools, and a full creator resources library — with much more on the way.

What We Stand For

Our core values

Everything we build is an expression of these four principles.

👥
Community First

Every product decision starts with a single question: does this make the community stronger? We don't build features to grow revenue — we build them to deepen real creative connections.

🌿
Authentic Growth

We believe in growing audiences that are genuinely interested, not inflated numbers. Every tool we offer is designed to build real engagement and lasting relationships between creators and their followers.

⚡
Creator Empowerment

Creators deserve more control over how they collaborate, how they earn, and how they grow. We put the power back in the hands of the people who make the content the world watches.

🔎
Transparency

From deal splits to platform fees to how our matching algorithm works — we believe in radical transparency. If you're using 100CLAN, you should understand exactly how it works and why.
