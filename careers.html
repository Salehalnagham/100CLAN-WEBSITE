How We Work

Our culture

Three principles that define how we show up every day.

🌍

Remote First

We are fully distributed across multiple countries and time zones. Async by default, video by exception. We believe great work happens everywhere — not just in an office.

🌟

Creator Obsessed

Every product decision, every strategy call, every line of code starts with a single question: does this make creators win? We think like creators because many of us are creators.

🚀

Move Fast

We ship weekly and iterate daily. Perfectionism is the enemy of momentum. We value speed with judgment — launch, learn, and improve relentlessly.
