Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers before you reach out.

How do I join 100CLAN as a creator?
+

Simply click "Join the Clan" on any page and fill out the creator registration form. We review every application and you'll receive a response within 48 hours. There's no minimum follower count, we welcome creators at all stages.

Is 100CLAN free to use?
+

Yes. The core features of 100CLAN, including creator matching, the collaboration hub, and community access, are free forever. We offer optional premium plans for creators who want advanced analytics, priority matching, and deal management tools.

How can brands or agencies partner with 100CLAN?
+

We love working with brands and agencies who want to connect with authentic creator communities. Use the contact form above with the subject "Partnership" and tell us a bit about your brand and goals. Our partnerships team will reach out within 2 business days.

What platforms does 100CLAN support?
+

100CLAN supports creators from all major platforms including Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X (Twitter), Snapchat, and podcasting platforms. Our matching algorithm considers platform overlap when suggesting collaborations to maximize reach.
