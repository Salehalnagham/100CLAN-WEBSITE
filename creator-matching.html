What We Analyze

12 data points.
One perfect match.

Our matching engine doesn't rely on follower count alone. Here's what drives your compatibility score.

🎨
Primary Signal

Niche Alignment

We map your content against 240+ niche subcategories to find creators in adjacent spaces that genuinely complement your content style and audience interests.

👥
Audience Signal

Audience Overlap Analysis

We analyze shared audience demographics, age, location, interests, to identify the ideal overlap range. Too much overlap means no new reach; too little means no resonance.

📈
Performance Signal

Engagement Rate

Raw follower counts are misleading. We weight engagement rate, likes, comments, shares, saves, far more heavily to find creators with genuinely active, responsive audiences.

📊
Growth Signal

Growth Trajectory

We track 90-day follower momentum and engagement trends. A creator on an upward curve brings exponential collab value that a stagnant large account cannot.

🎬
Content Signal

Content Style

Production quality, posting frequency, format preferences (Reels vs. long-form vs. static), and brand tone are all factored to ensure creative chemistry from day one.

🤝
Collab Signal

Collaboration History

Track record matters. We score past collab completion rate, feedback ratings, and the measured growth impact of previous partnerships to find reliable, proven collaborators.
