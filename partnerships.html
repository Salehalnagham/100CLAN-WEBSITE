Partnership Models

Find the right partnership

We offer three core partnership tracks designed to fit where you are and where you want to go.

Brand Partnerships

Connect your brand directly with the creators your audience already follows. Launch authentic campaigns at scale through our creator network.

  • Curated creator matching for your target demographic
  • Managed campaign workflows from brief to delivery
  • Full performance reporting and ROI tracking
  • Access to 12,000+ verified creators in 45+ countries
Agency Partnerships

White-label our collaboration tools for your own clients. Give your talent roster the infrastructure they need to collaborate, grow, and close deals, under your brand.

  • White-label platform access with your branding
  • Dedicated account manager and onboarding support
  • Revenue sharing on deals closed through the platform
  • Priority access to new features and beta programs
Platform Integrations

Build on top of 100CLAN through our developer API. Integrate creator data, collaboration workflows, and deal management into your own products.

  • RESTful API with comprehensive documentation
  • Webhooks for real-time collaboration event streaming
  • Sandbox environment for testing and development
  • Co-marketing opportunities for integration launches
