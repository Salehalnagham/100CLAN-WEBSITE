Key Facts Sheet

100CLAN at a glance

  • Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Dubai, UAE, with a remote-first team across 12 countries.
  • 12,000+ verified content creators active on the platform across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, and Snapchat.
  • Creators in 45+ countries spanning the Middle East, Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia.
  • Over 38,000 creator-to-creator collaborations facilitated since launch.
  • Average audience growth of 34% reported by creators within their first 90 days on the platform.
  • $2.8M+ in brand deal revenue managed through the platform's deal management tools.
  • Currently raising a Series A round to expand matching capabilities and enter new markets.
  • Supported content niches include lifestyle, tech, fitness, finance, gaming, travel, food, fashion, and beauty.
