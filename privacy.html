1. Introduction

Welcome to 100CLAN. 100CLAN FZ-LLC ("100CLAN," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at 100clan.com, use our mobile applications, or access any of our platform services (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please discontinue use of the Services immediately. We encourage you to read this policy carefully and contact us with any questions.

This policy applies to all users of 100CLAN globally. Where applicable, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection (PDPL), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws. If you are accessing our Services from a jurisdiction with specific data protection requirements, additional terms may apply as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, complete your creator profile, submit forms, communicate with us, or otherwise interact with the Services. This includes your name, email address, username, profile photograph, social media handles, platform follower counts, content niche preferences, location (country and city), and payment or billing information when you subscribe to a paid plan.

We automatically collect certain technical information when you access or use our Services. This includes your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, referring URLs, pages viewed, links clicked, session duration, and the date and time of your visit. We collect this information using cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies as further described in our Cookie Policy.

When you connect third-party social media accounts (such as Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or X) to your 100CLAN profile, we may collect publicly available information from those platforms including your profile data, follower counts, engagement metrics, and public content. We only collect the data necessary to provide the matching and analytics features of our platform and do so in accordance with those platforms' terms and our permissions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the 100CLAN platform and Services. Specifically, we use your data to: create and manage your account; power our creator matching algorithm; facilitate collaborations between creators; process payments and manage subscriptions; send you platform notifications, updates, and administrative messages; and respond to your support inquiries.

We use aggregated and anonymised data to generate analytics and insights that improve the platform experience for all users. For example, we may analyse collaboration outcomes across niche categories to improve our matching recommendations. We may also use your information to send you marketing communications about new features, partnership opportunities, and events, you may opt out of these at any time via your account settings or the unsubscribe link in any email.

We process your personal data on the following legal bases: (a) performance of a contract, processing necessary to provide the Services you have requested; (b) legitimate interests, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and platform improvements; (c) consent, marketing communications and optional data sharing; and (d) legal obligations, compliance with applicable law. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

4. Information Sharing

100CLAN does not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your private data with advertisers for behavioural targeting without your explicit consent. Your creator profile information (name, handle, niche, platform presence, and publicly listed metrics) is visible to other verified creators on the platform as part of the core matching and community features. You control which additional details appear on your public profile through your privacy settings.

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the platform. These include cloud hosting providers (AWS, Google Cloud), payment processors (Stripe), email service providers, analytics tools, and customer support software. All service providers are contractually obligated to handle your data in a manner consistent with this policy and to implement appropriate security measures. We do not permit service providers to use your data for their own purposes.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or regulatory authority, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of 100CLAN, our users, or the public. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, and we will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our platform prior to any such change.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate the Services, remember your preferences (including language settings), maintain your login session, and analyse platform usage. Some cookies are strictly necessary for the platform to function; others are used for performance analytics or to show you relevant content within the platform. We do not use tracking cookies for cross-site advertising.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences or through our Cookie Preference Centre, accessible from the footer of any page. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Services. For full details on the specific cookies we use, their purpose, and their duration, please review our Cookie Policy.

We may use third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our platform. These services collect information sent by your browser, including the pages you visit and other analytics data. Google's use of this data is governed by its own privacy policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal information within 90 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records (such as billing and transaction records, which we retain for up to 7 years in accordance with applicable financial regulations).

Certain aggregated, anonymised data derived from your use of the platform may be retained indefinitely for analytics and product improvement purposes. This data cannot be used to identify you individually. Data stored in our backup systems will be purged according to our standard backup rotation schedule, typically within 90 days of the deletion of the primary record.

If you request deletion of your account and personal data, we will action your request within 30 days and confirm deletion by email. You may submit a deletion request through your account settings under "Privacy & Data" or by contacting us at privacy@100clan.com. Please note that some information may remain in archived communications or records that other users have retained in connection with your collaborations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information: (a) the right to access, you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you; (b) the right to rectification, you may ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data; (c) the right to erasure, you may request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions; (d) the right to restrict processing, in certain circumstances, you may ask us to limit how we use your data; (e) the right to data portability, you may request your data in a commonly used machine-readable format; and (f) the right to object, you may object to processing based on our legitimate interests.

If you are a resident of California, you have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed, the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights. To submit a CCPA request, contact us at privacy@100clan.com with the subject line "CCPA Request."

To exercise any of your rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact Us section below. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days. In some cases, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request. If we are unable to fulfil your request for legal or operational reasons, we will explain why. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you are unsatisfied with our response.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, or destruction. These measures include TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit, AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest, access controls and role-based permissions, regular security audits and penetration testing, and multi-factor authentication for administrative access to our systems.

While we take the security of your data seriously, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant regulatory authorities as required by applicable law, typically within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

You play an important role in keeping your data secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your 100CLAN account, enable two-factor authentication in your account settings, and be cautious about phishing attempts. We will never ask for your password via email or unsolicited phone calls. If you suspect your account has been compromised, please contact us at security@100clan.com immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

100CLAN is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are under 16, please do not attempt to register for an account or submit any personal information through the Services. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 16, we will delete that information immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@100clan.com. We will investigate and, where appropriate, delete the information and terminate any account associated with that child. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) for users in the United States and equivalent laws in other jurisdictions.

For users between the ages of 16 and 18, we recommend that a parent or guardian review this Privacy Policy before the minor uses the Services. Some features of the platform, particularly those related to commercial deal management, may require users to confirm that they are of the age of majority in their jurisdiction or have appropriate guardian consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. When we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email (to the address associated with your account) and by posting a prominent notice on our website at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The updated policy will be accessible at all times at 100clan.com/privacy.

Your continued use of the Services after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the changes, you should stop using the Services and request account deletion before the effective date. For minor, non-material changes (such as typographical corrections or clarifications that do not affect how we process your data), we may update the policy without advance notice.

We maintain an archive of previous versions of this Privacy Policy. If you would like to review a prior version, please contact us at privacy@100clan.com and we will provide the requested version within 5 business days. The date at the top of this policy ("Last Updated") indicates when the most recent changes were made.
